Webinar:Introduction to CodeWars

How to build a Project from scratch in two hours was an exhilarating and enlightening event hosted by Algozenith GNDU Chapter, on October 20, 2023, a student-led community of coding enthusiasts and innovators. The event showcased how a team of two students, led by a senior mentor, can create a functional and impressive project in just two hours using various tools and techniques. The event was graced by the presence of *Manas Gupta*, a senior member of Algozenith GNDU Chapter and an experienced developer, who shared his expertise and insights with the participants.
